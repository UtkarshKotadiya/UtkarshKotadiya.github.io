Store History

Paramount Jewelers' history dates back to the early 2000s, when Mr. Frank first got into the business through a kiosk in the mall called GoldNJewels. After years of hard work and dedication, Mr. Frank opened a full fledged store in the mall in 2009. And since then, Paramount Jewelers has been serving clients from all over with the utmost dedication and hospitality. Many of our clients exclusively shop jewelery only at Paramount Jewelers because of the outstanding customer service and the focus on building a relationship with the client and not seeing the process as a mere business transaction.
